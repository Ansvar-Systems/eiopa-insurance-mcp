## Recommended Verification Workflow

Before relying on any regulatory information retrieved through this server:

1. **Identify the relevant EIOPA guideline or technical standard** using this server's search tools
2. **Retrieve the official document** directly from the EIOPA publications portal at https://www.eiopa.europa.eu/
3. **Confirm the version and status** — check whether the document has been revised or superseded
4. **Read the full original text** — do not rely solely on summaries or excerpts
5. **Check for Q&A and guidance** — EIOPA may have issued supplementary Q&A on the same topic
6. **Consult qualified legal or compliance counsel** for interpretation and application to your specific facts
